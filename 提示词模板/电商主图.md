# 电商主图提示词模板

适用于淘宝、天猫、拼多多、京东、抖音、快手、小红书、微信小店等商品首图和轮播图。默认目标是高点击、高转化、平台合规。

## 高转化成品主图

```text
Use case: ads-marketing
Asset type: {平台} ecommerce product main image, square 1:1, finished image with native Chinese text.

Primary request:
Create a high-click, high-conversion ecommerce main image for {商品}. The final image must be generated directly by the image model, including product photo, headline, selling-point tags, badge, and CTA text. Do not leave blank spaces for later editing.

Canvas:
{尺寸}, square ecommerce main image, polished {平台} style.

Subject:
{商品} as the dominant product, clearly recognizable, accurate to the actual item, premium and appetizing/appealing. Show {核心卖点}. Product should fill about 70%-85% of the image unless platform rules require otherwise.

Composition:
Large product foreground, clean high-impact layout, strong visual hierarchy, safe margins around all text, no cropped product or cropped characters. Use a clear headline area, 2-4 short selling tags, one badge, and one bottom CTA ribbon if appropriate for {平台}.

Visual style:
Premium ecommerce advertising, clean, bright, high trust, strong click appeal. Style should match {品类}: fresh and appetizing for food, tactile and detailed for fashion/accessories, practical and clear for household goods.

Text:
Render only the following Simplified Chinese text, verbatim, crisp and readable. No extra text.
Main headline: "{主标题}"
Subheadline: "{副标题}"
Tags: "{标签1}" "{标签2}" "{标签3}"
Badge: "{角标}"
CTA: "{CTA}"

Constraints:
No watermark, no random words, no gibberish, no English filler, no QR code, no phone number, no external platform guidance, no unauthorized logo, no misleading quantity, no distorted product, no cropped text. Avoid {禁用内容}. Keep all important elements inside safe margins.
```

## 无字商品基础图

用于先生成干净商品底图，再让 image generation 继续编辑出成品图。只有用户明确允许分阶段时使用。

```text
Use case: ads-marketing
Asset type: clean ecommerce product base image, no text.

Create a premium photorealistic ecommerce product image for {商品}. No text, no logos, no watermark, no labels, no random packaging words.

Canvas:
{尺寸}, square 1:1.

Subject:
{商品}, large and clear, accurate shape and material, showing {核心卖点}. Product fills the center and lower area, with clean negative space reserved for later native AI text editing.

Scene:
{背景/场景}. Clean, premium, platform-friendly. Use natural props only if they support the product and do not imply extra included items.

Lighting:
Bright soft studio light or natural daylight, clear details, polished but realistic.

Constraints:
No text, no logo, no watermark, no people faces, no misleading accessories, no distorted product, no random symbols, no messy background.
```

## 平台风格提醒

| 平台 | 提醒 |
| --- | --- |
| 淘宝/拼多多 | 可以更强促销感，但文字要少而大 |
| 天猫/京东 | 品牌感、清晰度、可信度优先 |
| 小红书 | 减少硬促销，多生活方式和真实种草感 |
| 抖音/快手 | 缩略图识别强，卖点直接，真实感重要 |
| Amazon | 主图不要用此成品文案模板，优先用白底图模板 |
