# 外卖菜品图提示词模板

适用于美团、饿了么、京东外卖等菜品图、套餐图、分类图。核心是“真实、清楚、有食欲”，不要把普通菜品图做成夸张广告海报。

## 单品菜品图

```text
Use case: food delivery menu image
Asset type: {平台} dish photo.

Primary request:
Create a realistic appetizing menu image for the dish "{菜品名称}". The image should look like a real delivered or dine-in dish from a restaurant, suitable for {平台} dish listing.

Canvas:
{尺寸}, recommended 800x600, horizontal 4:3.

Subject:
The dish "{菜品名称}" as the main subject. Portion size, ingredients, side dishes, bowl/plate/container, and presentation should match the actual sold item: {实际内容}. Main dish should occupy about 60%-80% of the frame.

Composition:
Clean 45-degree or overhead food photography, dish centered, safe margins, no clutter. Use simple tableware or delivery container appropriate to the dish. Make the food clear in thumbnail view.

Lighting:
Bright natural food photography, appetizing but realistic color, no heavy filter, no excessive shine.

Text:
No text unless explicitly requested.

Constraints:
No price, no discount, no delivery-fee text, no QR code, no phone number, no exaggerated portion, no ingredients not included, no fake brand logo, no watermark, no random text. Avoid {禁用内容}.
```

## 套餐图

```text
Use case: food delivery combo image
Asset type: {平台} combo meal image.

Create a realistic appetizing image for combo "{套餐名称}".

Canvas:
{尺寸}, recommended 800x600, horizontal 4:3.

Subject:
Show every included item clearly: {套餐内容}. Keep quantities accurate and visually balanced. Do not add extra drinks, snacks, sauces, garnish, or gifts unless they are included in sale.

Composition:
Main dish is the visual anchor, side items arranged naturally around it, clean table or delivery packaging, thumbnail-friendly.

Lighting:
Bright, natural, appetizing, realistic.

Constraints:
No misleading quantity, no omitted included item, no extra item, no random packaging words, no price/discount text, no platform-incompatible claims.
```

## 外卖活动图/分类图

```text
Use case: food delivery store marketing image
Asset type: {平台} category or promotion image.

Create a clean food delivery marketing image for "{主题}".

Canvas:
{尺寸}.

Subject:
Feature representative dishes: {菜品}. Food should look realistic and consistent with the store's actual menu.

Text:
Render only this Simplified Chinese text, crisp and readable:
"{主标题}"
"{副标题}"

Layout:
Keep text large and centered in safe area. Food remains clear and appetizing. Avoid crowding.

Constraints:
No false discount, no QR code, no phone number, no external platform guidance, no unrealistic portions, no random text.
```

## 外卖图常用角度

| 菜品 | 推荐角度 |
| --- | --- |
| 炒菜/盖饭 | 45 度或微俯拍 |
| 面/粉/汤 | 45 度，注意汤面反光和蒸汽 |
| 炸物/小吃 | 45 度，突出酥脆纹理 |
| 饮品 | 正面或 45 度，杯身完整 |
| 套餐 | 俯拍或 45 度，所有内容清楚 |
